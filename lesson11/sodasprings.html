Get Ready to Fun Run!

In Soda Springs we are looking at our highest temperatures of the year this week. As we get into the 60s for the first time since fall, everyone is excited to get outside and get moving. We have seen more runners on the sidewalks in the last couple of days than we've seen people outside since December! Signups for this year's Soda Springs Marathon and Half Marathon have tripled in the last 2 weeks and even the kids are getting excited for our first annual Kid's Soda Fun Run! The fun run will be 1 mile and all who participate will recieve a tshirt and a bottle of soda bottled from our famous soda springs. Sign up now for what will surely be the highlight of your summer!
