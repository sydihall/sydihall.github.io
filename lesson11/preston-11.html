Spring is Blossoming

Preston is home of hundreds of cherry blossom trees and we are coming up on the most beautiful time of the year for Preston residents and visitors. Every spring the town turns into a magical blossom land. The trees seem to sparkle with their beautiful pink and white flowers. This year the annual Blossom Festival will be on May 7th in the City Square Park. Bring your family and soak in the season of flowers and magic.
