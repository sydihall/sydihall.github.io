Fishing Season Now Open!

Fish Haven has been having some extremely warm weather recently! What does this mean for residents and visitors of Fish Haven? Fishing Season is on its way! As the weather warms up, we start preparing our poles and tackle boxes to start our season out. Fish Haven isn't just the name of our little town, it really is a haven where you can fish all you want! This year we are expecting record numbers of fish coming to be caught by you! The vibrant colors of their scales will catch in the sunlight directly below the water and you'll feel like you're in a perfect, well, haven.
