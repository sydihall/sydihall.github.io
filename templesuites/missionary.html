Full-Time Missionary Accommodations

Start adjusting to sharing a room and sleeping in a bed right next to your companion's bed! Our 'Comp Prep Suites' are perfect for your first trip to the temple with your family. The rooms are nice but humble and have very little room to have your own space. We strive to help our future missionaries be as prepared as they can be to enter the mission field!

Our Comp Prep Suites have nice bathrooms with great lighting to help you get ready and really look like a representative of the church. The sinks are up close to the mirror so you can get a nice close shave or get that natural look with your makeup.
