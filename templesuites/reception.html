Wedding Receptions

Our beautiful reception halls are available as a convenient option to host a party after your temple sealings. The rooms are large with plenty of room for tables, dancing, and toasts!

Temple Inn and Suites Catering is good for more than just our complimentary breakfast! We offer catering for events of all sizes! Our delicious hors d'oeuvres highlight the amazing entrees and desserts that we can make a part of your evening. Call for details on our rotating menu!

Work with Our Event Planners to Make Your Day Effortless!

We have onsite event planners that can help take care of everything and coordinate with catering to make everything go smoothly. We have 2 great planners who will help you to plan the perfect event!

Hanna Durant

Hanna has been with Temple Inn and Suites for 4 years now! She is great at capturing a vision and creating some magic! She specializes in wedding receptions.

Courtney Flaw

Courtney has over 10 years of experience in event planning and we are happy to have her as part of our team here at Temple Inn and Suites. She knows how to throw big events or small family gatherings, depending on what you want to do!
